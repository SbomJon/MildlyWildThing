Mildly Wild Thing

A blog about the ins and outs of a strange mind


I always wanted to create a blog to talk about something, but I never had the ability to do so.
Well. I still don't have, but now I'm trying to learn the basics so I can get started.

One question I had right off the bat that was kind of annoying to get over was the question of what to write about... should I write about photography, perhaps, since it's a beloved hobby of mine? Or write about Architecture, which is my field in college?
No! I wanted something more… remarkable.

Don't get me wrong, I'm not saying architecture or photography aren't remarkable subjects; I just want to make my mark with something more personal. Like a captain's journal or a mission report, in which I would relate my experiences - or should I say, my "craziences" — and hide in plain sight this savagery that is life. The life from my point of view.
